📏 Online Ruler: Precise 1:1 Screen Measurement & Physical Calibration Tool
—— A lightweight, high-precision professional browser extension that transforms your screen into a real-world physical ruler and protractor.

-----------------------------------

Do you often struggle to obtain accurate measurements when sizing objects on your screen due to varying resolutions and zoom levels? "Online Ruler" is designed specifically to solve this common frustration. Utilizing unique physical calibration technology, it dynamically renders precise 1:1 scales based on your monitor's actual PPI (Pixels Per Inch), providing you with real-world-accurate measurement references.

Whether you are a designer, an engineer, or a student learning about geometry, this extension offers you the most intuitive and reliable digital measurement solution.

🌟 Core Features
-----------------------------------
✨ Physical-Level Calibration:
    Align the tool with standard physical objects—like a credit card or A4 paper—to automatically calculate your screen's exact PPI.

📏 Multi-Tool Support:
    Add multiple rulers or protractors on the same page for complex comparative measurements and geometric positioning.

📏 Dual Measurement Modes:
    Standalone Window Mode: Opens a clean, "always-on-top" ruler window.
    Page Overlay Mode: Displays floating rulers or protractors directly over any webpage with adjustable transparency.

🔄 360° Arbitrary Rotation:
    Use the rotation handle to adjust the angle in real-time. Rulers support snapping to key angles (45°/90°) and display a real-time angle badge.

🔄 Zero Offset & Reset (Ruler):
    Mimics the protective "zero-point" edge and allows setting a custom starting offset. Includes a one-click "Reset" function.

🎨 Realistic Aesthetics & Materials:
    Rulers support Wood, Plastic, or Metal textures with dynamic SVG filters. Plastic materials offer several color options.

📏 Comprehensive Scale Support:
    Metric: Centimeter (CM) and Millimeter (MM).
    Imperial: Inch scales, high-precision down to 1/16 of an inch.

🔄 Flexible Interaction Logic:
    Full-Edge Resizing: Drag any point along the end of the ruler to adjust its length.
    Real-time Synchronization: Changes to materials, colors, or settings are applied instantly.

💾 Privacy & Local Storage:
    All calibration data is stored exclusively within your local browser.

📖 Instructions
-----------------------------------
1. First-time Use: Click the extension icon to enter "Calibration Mode."
2. Alignment: Select a reference object, place it against your screen, and adjust the slider until the mark aligns perfectly.
3. Start Measuring: Click "Add Ruler to Current Page" or "Add Protractor."
4. Change Style: Use the toolbar icons to adjust materials, colors, or transparency.
5. Move & Rotate: Drag the tool to move it, and use the round handle to rotate it.

🎨 Design Philosophy
-----------------------------------
Precision Above All: Built upon the formula `pixelsPerMm = ppi / 25.4`.
Toolkit Thinking: Combining rulers and protractors for UI debugging, blueprint checking, and education.
Instant Usability: Wide drag handles and clear zero marks to make it effortless.
